My First Blog Post

April 29, 2017

The 1st module of the backend engineering program at Turing School of Software and Design is no laughing matter! I knew it was going to be a challenge, but this may have been the hardest thing I've ever done in my adult life. I know I have a penchant for exaggeration and hyperbole, but this was painful, excrutiating, spirit-breaking and ultimately rewarding.

It blows my mind to think about how much I learned and how far I have progressed in the span of only six weeks. I especially never thought such leaps in cognitive development were possible at such an...accelerated age...
